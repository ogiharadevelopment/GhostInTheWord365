Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 1, 2025

1. Introduction

GhostInTheWord ("we", "our", or "the Add-in") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Microsoft Word add-in.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally

GhostInTheWord stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Saved Formats: Text formatting preferences you save (font name, size, color, alignment, etc.)
  • Language Preference: Your selected language (Japanese or English)
  • Premium Status: Whether you have upgraded to the premium version

Note: All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We do not transmit this data to any external servers.

2.2 Document Content

GhostInTheWord accesses your Word document content only to read and apply formatting. We do not store, transmit, or share any document content.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Document content or text
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • IP addresses or location data

3. How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally is used solely for:

  • Saving and applying your preferred text formats
  • Maintaining your language preference
  • Managing premium feature access

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored in your browser's localStorage
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not upload or store any data on external servers
  • Device-Specific: Data is only accessible on the device where you use the add-in
  • No Encryption: Data is stored in plain text in localStorage as it contains no sensitive information

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. GhostInTheWord operates entirely locally and does not transmit any information to external services.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by:

  • Clearing your browser's localStorage
  • Uninstalling the add-in
  • Clearing your browser cache and cookies

6.2 Data Export

Since all data is stored locally, you can access it through your browser's developer tools.

7. Children's Privacy

GhostInTheWord does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The add-in is designed for general use and does not require age verification.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

9. Microsoft Office Add-in Permissions

GhostInTheWord requests the following permissions:

  • ReadWriteDocument: To read and modify text formatting in your Word documents

These permissions are used solely for the add-in's core functionality and not for data collection.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
